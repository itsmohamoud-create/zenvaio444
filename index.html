FAQ

Everything You Need to Know About Zen

And how it can help your business grow.

How do you customise the solution?

We start with a strategy call to understand your business, workflows, and goals. Then we configure your AI agent with your brand voice, qualifying questions, and integration requirements.

What is the typical implementation timeline?

Most systems go live within 14 days. Complex integrations may take up to 21 days. We handle all the technical setup and testing — you don't need to do anything.

Do you support multiple languages?

Yes! Zen supports English, Spanish, French, and Mandarin. Additional languages available on request for Full Stack clients.

What if my business has unique integration needs?

We integrate with 50+ tools including HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, Pipedrive, Monday, Calendly, and custom APIs. If we don't have it, we'll build it.

Can Zen transfer calls to a real person?

Absolutely. Zen can warm-transfer qualified leads to your sales team, or escalate urgent calls based on your rules.

How does billing work?

Simple monthly billing with no hidden fees. Starter plans are month-to-month. Growth and Full Stack plans offer annual discounts.
